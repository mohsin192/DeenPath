What kind of resources are available?

Quran

Qur'an Page
Which text and translations are used?
Uthmani script with multiple trusted translations (English/Urdu). You can switch translation in the reader.
Is audio recitation available?
Yes. Play/pause per ayah, repeat, and choose from available reciters.
Can I bookmark ayahs and add notes?
Yes. Sign in to save bookmarks, notes, and your last read position across devices.

Namaz Time

Prayer Times Page
How are prayer times calculated?
By your location plus your chosen method (MWL, ISNA, Umm al-Qura, etc.) and juristic school for Asr.
Can I adjust angles or method?
Yes. You can customize method, twilight angles, and minute offsets per prayer.
Do you provide adhan notifications?
Yes, via web push/email where supported. Enable notifications after signing in.

Calendar

Hijri & Gregorian
Do you show both Hijri and Gregorian dates?
Yes. You can toggle views and apply local moon-sighting or Umm al-Qura adjustments.
Can I export events to my calendar app?
Yes. Download .ics files and import to Google, Apple, or Outlook calendars.
Are key Islamic dates highlighted?
Yes—Ramadan, Eid al-Fitr, Eid al-Adha, Ashura, and other notable days are clearly marked.

Counter (Tasbeeh)

Dhikr Tool
How does the digital counter work?
Tap/click to increase the count. Set targets (e.g., 33/33/34) and reset when needed.
Will my counts be saved?
Yes, when logged in, your counts and presets sync to your account and devices.
Is there vibration or sound feedback?
Optional haptic/sound feedback is available on supported devices (can be turned on/off).

Find Mosque

Find Mosque Page
How does the Find Mosque feature work?
It uses your location to display nearby mosques with maps and directions.
Do I need to enable location services?
Yes. Location access must be enabled for accurate results.
Can I search for mosques in another city?
Yes. You can manually enter a city or address to find mosques in that area.
Are mosque details verified?
We rely on community submissions and public databases. Users can report errors to help improve accuracy.

Qibla Direction

Qibla Page
How does the Qibla finder work?
It uses your device’s compass and location to show the exact direction of the Kaaba.
Is it accurate indoors?
Accuracy may vary indoors. For best results, use it outdoors or near a window and calibrate your device's compass.
Can I use it offline?
Yes, once loaded, the Qibla direction works offline using your device's compass.
